With over 10 years of design experience and 50 digital launches under his belt, Toby Grubb served as a Creative Director at Instrument before starting This.Studio in Portland, Oregon. He provides insights and visual guidance to active global brands, including Nike, Burton, Black Diamond, Red Bull and Specialized. His unique blend of energy and vision fuels collaboration. He is constantly making, building and occasionally, sleeping.

Toby is also an intrepid adventurer and photographer. Whether mountain biking down volcanoes, racing motorcycles through the Italian autostrade, shark diving in the Pacific or backcountry snowboarding in New Zealand – he’s compelled to find and document the best life has to offer. This thirst for life is evident in his work.
